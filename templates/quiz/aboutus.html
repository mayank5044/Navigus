About Us !


At Navigus, we realise the potential of resolving this colossal problem plaguing the stage with unfortunate young careers going astray. The mission of Navigus is laid on making career-course-college decision a concrete science. We plan to achieve this by deploying a cutting edge Artificial Intelligence and Data Science to create a virtual replica of students, identify their sweet spot between will, skill and motivations and map them to their best fit course-college-career combination - thus providing them a platform for self discovery at an early stage of life.
